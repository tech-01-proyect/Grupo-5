Digital Art: A Short Introduction

Digital art is a form of artistic expression that uses technology to create, modify, and share artwork. It started in the second half of the 20th century with computers and has grown with new software, artificial intelligence, and digital tools like graphic tablets.

There are many types of digital art, such as digital painting, 3D modeling, animation, generative art, and interactive art. Digital art has changed how artists work, making it easier to experiment and share their creations with people around the world.
